Elections

The CSSS holds multiple elections a year to make sure the best people are helping our members. If you'd like to get even more involved with the CS community then running for a position is a great way to do that. Full rules for elected positions are in the

Eligibility

  • The President, Vice-President, Treasurer, and SFSS Council Representative must be doing a major, minor, or honours program offered by the School of Computing Science.
  • Both the First Year Representatives must be doing a major, minor, or honours program and be in their first year of study.
  • No member of the CSSS may hold more than one executive position at a time.

These executives hold office from May 1st to April 30th of the following year, except for the Executives at Large, who are voted in at the start of every semester and serve until the end of that semester.
